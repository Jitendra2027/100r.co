our philosophy

We build software following these rules:

accessibility

We design our applications to work on a wide range of platforms, from web technologies to portable terminal versions, built from C99 and assembly.

no drm

We design our tools for the future and will never release them behind any form of Digital Rights Management.

lean

We build simple tools with the few dependencies and design them to tackle specific tasks.

freedom

We release free and open-source programs so that they can be studied, inspected and repaired by anyone, anywhere.

linux

We build for Linux-first.
